About Me

I am a 3rd year Software Technology student from De La Salle University who enjoys solving problems through code. I am fueled by curiosity and a desire to learn new technologies that will further help me discover new ways to create new solutions. I thrive on challenges, never running out of things to further my knowledge, and encourages me to create.

Outside of programming, I enjoy reading, playing video games, and drawing.

Given my PCK and APQ results, I've realized that I am conventional - someone who likes to work following set procedures and routines. I prefer working when there are precise standards rather than judging things by myself, which admittedly I am very indecisive about. Moreover, I have also learned that I am also investigative - someone who likes to work activities that are related to ideas and thinking, more than physical activity. Some work importance values that were considered my top include support, achievement, and relationships. For one, support means that I prefer having a support role in work such as being a credit analyst, aviation inspector, etc. On the other hand, achievement means that I value jobs that value achievement like sketch artists, surveyors, and computer programmers. On the other hand, relationships mean that I value jobs that value relationships. Subsequent ot this, I have also learned that I am mostly proficient in my cognitive abilities, i.e. arithmetic reasoning, verbal ability, spatial ability, computation, clerical perception, and form perception.
